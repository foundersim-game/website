Do you have what it takes to build a billion-dollar Unicorn? From Zero to IPO!

Welcome to Founder Sim, the most realistic startup simulator and business tycoon game on the market. Step into the shoes of a Silicon Valley CEO and navigate the high-stakes journey from a garage-built MVP to a massive IPO. 

🚀 BUILD YOUR LEGACY
Start as a solo founder with nothing but an idea and some focus energy. Use your technical skills to build features, refine your product quality, and launch your Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to the world. In this business tycoon game, every decision matters—from the code you write to the first user you acquire.

📈 STRATEGIC GROWTH & MARKETING
Getting users isn't just about luck; it's about strategy. Master different Go-To-Market (GTM) approaches:
• Product-Led Growth (PLG): Build viral features that let the product sell itself.
• Sales-Led Growth (SLG): Hire a high-octane sales team to close massive B2B enterprise deals.
• Marketing Machine: Run PR campaigns, dominate organic social media, and build an SEO content machine to grow your MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) automatically.

👥 HIRE & MANAGE YOUR TEAM
You can’t reach Unicorn status alone. Recruit top talent across Engineering, Marketing, and Sales departments. 
• Engineers: Boost product quality and manage tech debt.
• Marketers: Increase brand awareness and growth rate.
• Sales Reps: Close the big deals and drive revenue.
• C-Suite: Hire a CFO to optimize your bills or a Co-Founder to share the burnout burden.

💰 FINANCE & FUNDRAISING
Manage your company’s cash flow with surgical precision. Watch your Burn Rate and monitor your Runway carefully. Need a cash injection? Pitch to venture capitalists for a seed round or Series A. But beware—taking investor money means giving up Equity. Can you stay the majority owner until the final exit?

⚠️ REAL-WORLD CHALLENGES
Founder Sim isn't just an idle clicker; it’s a deep management simulation. Face realistic events that can make or break your company:
• Handle "Server Meltdowns" caused by too much tech debt.
• Navigate market shifts and competitor moves.
• Manage your own Focus Energy and avoid burnout.
• Balance product reliability with the need for speed.

🏆 THE EXIT STRATEGY
Your ultimate goal is the "Big Exit." Will you be acquired by a tech giant or take your company public via an IPO? Your final score depends on your final valuation, your remaining equity, and your cash in the bank. 

KEY FEATURES:
• Realistic Startup Mechanics: MRR, CAC, Tech Debt, Equity, and Burn Rate.
• Department Power: Scale your company by hiring and managing diverse teams.
• Deep Strategy: Choose between PLG and SLG strategies based on your product.
• Dynamic Events: No two playthroughs are the same.
• Retro-Modern UI: A beautiful, data-rich interface designed for aspiring moguls.

Download Founder Sim today and start your journey from a garage to the stock exchange. The next great tech tycoon empire starts with YOU.
